Named templates in Helm are reusable code blocks that you define once (usually in _helpers.tpl) and call multiple times across your chart.

They let you avoid repetition and keep your templates clean.

✅ Where Do We Define Them?

Always inside:
charts/mychart/templates/_helpers.tpl

Example: Standard Labels Template
✅ _helpers.tpl
{{- define "mychart.labels" -}}
app.kubernetes.io/name: {{ .Chart.Name }}
app.kubernetes.io/instance: {{ .Release.Name }}
app.kubernetes.io/version: {{ .Chart.Version }}
app.kubernetes.io/managed-by: {{ .Release.Service }}
{{- end }}

✅ deployment.yaml
metadata:
  labels:
    {{- include "mychart.labels" . | nindent 4 }}

This injects all labels properly indented.
